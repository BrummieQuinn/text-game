You Choose The Right Door

You approach the door on the right,
when you open it and find a bright room with a giant web.
You walk inside and see a spider crawling on the ceiling.
It looks at you and says "Hello!". You scream and say
"What the hell? You can talk?". The spider nods and says "Yes, I can.
And I'm very lonely. Do you want to be my friend?". You decide to try
to escape the spider's web by making up some excuses.

Too late!
Your stuck, seeing your struggle, the spider smiles showing wicked sharp
teeth. It begins to rapidly make its way towards you.
