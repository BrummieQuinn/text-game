You have been summoned to a haunted mansion by a mysterious letter.
You wonder who could have sent it and what they want from you.
You decide to go anyway

Who are you?

A. Rogue or B. Necromancer?

If you choose Rogue, you can sneak around and use daggers and traps,
but you have to rely on your skills and luck. You hope to find some
treasure in the mansion, or at least some clues about the letter.

If you choose Necromancer, you can summon undead minions to fight
for you, but you have to deal with the smell and the moral implications.
You hope to find some secrets in the mansion, or at least some new allies.
